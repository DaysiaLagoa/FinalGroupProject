Activities


Here at Parents Care we plan a host of activities to stimulate all the censes threw open, constructive play, utilizing music, using small and gross motor skills, physical activities and movement to stimulate learning.

We are one of the largest parenting support group in the capital district. bringing local family together in our local neighborhood to try new things, enjoying fun activities and making new friends.

We meet once a week for socials with moms,dads,and children.

We offer a new calendar for each month with fun activities and play dates in each corner of the local area.
